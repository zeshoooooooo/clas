Eligibility Criteria

  • Authentic: Your account must represent a real person, registered business or entity.
  • Unique: Your account must be the unique presence of the entity it represents.
  • Complete: Your account must be public and have a bio, profile photo, and at least one post.
  • Notable: Your account must represent a well-known, often searched person, brand or entity.
  • Active: Your account must be active , with regular posts and engagement.
